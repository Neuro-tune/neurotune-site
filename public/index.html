Commands

🎵 Main Commands

  • 📀 /play — Play a track or playlist
  • ⏭️ /skip — Skip the current track
  • ⏹️ /stop — Stop and clear the queue
  • ⏸️ /pause — Pause playback
  • ▶️ /resume — Resume playback
  • 📄 /queue — Show queue with buttons
  • 🔀 /shuffle — Shuffle queue
  • 🔁 /repeat — Repeat mode
  • 🧹 /clear — Clear the queue

🎛️ Track Management

  • ❌ /remove — Remove a track by number
  • 🔃 /move — Move a track in the queue

📂 Playlists

  • ➕ /playlist add — Add track to your playlist
  • ➖ /playlist remove — Remove track from your playlist
  • ✏️ /playlist rename — Rename your playlist
  • 📋 /playlist list — Show your playlists

🤖 Smart Features

  • 🎚️ /autodj — Auto‑DJ: endless music
  • 🎭 /mood — Generate music by mood

👤 Profile & Settings

  • 🧾 /profile — View music stats
  • 💎 /buypremium — Buy/extend Premium
  • 🎼 /setdefaultgenre — Set favorite genre
  • 🎭 /setdefaultmood — Set default mood

ℹ️ More

  • 📚 /help — Show this menu again

FAQ

How do I invite Neuro•Tune?

Click the "Invite" button in the nav or use /invite.

Which platforms are supported?

Supports Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud, Deezer, Apple Music, and more.

How can I apply filters?

Use /filter <name> with names like bassboost, nightcore, 8D.

When will Premium release?

Soon! Stay tuned for updates on our Discord.
